uint16_t sx_truncation_profile_mirror_cfg::truncate_size

Truncation size: Units of 1B, Granularity of 4B, Min value: 64 Bytes, in case Parsing-Base masking is used the max truncation TRUNCATION_PROFILE_PARSING_BASED_MASKING_OFFSET_MAX bytes

◆ parsing_base_masking_en

boolean_t sx_truncation_profile_mirror_cfg::parsing_base_masking_en

Indication weather masking (zeroing) is enable. To enable Parsing-Base masking, truncation shell also be configured with truncate_size up to TRUNCATION_PROFILE_PARSING_BASED_MASKING_OFFSET_MAX (no Masking without truncation) Egress Mirror isn't support Masking Parsing Base.
